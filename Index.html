Church History

The History of CSI PASTORATE CHURCH MANGARAI (ESTD.1867) is located in between Karungal to Puthukadai Road. It is one of the biggest pastorate churches in the Karungal District church. This channel is used for all the activities of CSI Pastorate Church Mangarai. May God bless you all...
