Key Contributions of CSI Pastorate Church Mangarai

Church Renovation

Major structural upgrades and beautification of the sanctuary to reflect the spiritual dignity of the church.

Community Outreach

Reaching the underprivileged with food drives, education support, and medical camps.

Education Ministry

Supporting students with scholarships, coaching, and Christian value education programs.

Health Camps

Organizing regular health checkups and awareness programs for all age groups.

Bible Studies & Fellowship

Weekly gatherings for prayer, learning the Word of God, and spiritual mentoring.

Missionary Support

Financial and spiritual support for missionary work across Tamil Nadu and beyond.

Women's Fellowship

Empowering women through prayer meetings, counseling, and vocational training.

Sunday School

Nurturing children in Christian values with music, stories, and Scripture memorization.

Charity & Fundraising

Raising funds to support disaster victims, orphans, and local community needs.

Youth Ministry

Guiding young minds with leadership training, worship nights, and social outreach.

Spiritual Counseling

Providing guidance and support for personal, emotional, and faith-based challenges.

Infrastructure Development

Constructing and maintaining halls, restrooms, and facilities for the growing congregation.
